About Me

Sai Krish

Student · Athlete · Chess Enthusiast

Hi, I'm Sai Krish — born in 2016 and currently finishing 4th Grade at DAV School, Pallikaranai, Chennai. By June 2026, I'll be stepping into 5th Grade. I'm a playful, energetic kid who loves sports as much as I love solving tough maths problems.

I started my school journey at AKG Public School, where I studied from LKG through 2nd Grade, before moving to DAV School. Academics come naturally to me, and I always aim to score high marks while still finding plenty of time for the games and hobbies I love.

My goals are big: I want to bring home an Olympic gold medal in chess and lift the T20 World Cup for India as an all-rounder cricketer one day.
